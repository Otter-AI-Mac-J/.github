## 💡 Overview

<a href="#otterAi">Otter AI</a> is an AI-powered meeting transcription and note-taking application that automatically converts spoken meeting content into searchable, shareable text — capturing the complete record of meetings, calls, and conversations that participants would otherwise need to manually document.

The <a href="#otterAi">real-time transcription</a> converts speech to text as it happens — words appear on screen within seconds of being spoken, giving participants a live running transcript of the meeting while it is in progress. This real-time visibility allows participants to follow the discussion without full attention, verify what was said moments ago without asking for repetition, and catch their name and action items as they are assigned. The <a href="#otterAi">speaker identification</a> labels each transcript segment with the speaker's name — distinguishing between multiple participants in the same meeting so the transcript reads as a dialogue with attributed statements rather than an undifferentiated stream of text. Speaker identification works by learning voice profiles, improving attribution accuracy across meetings as the system becomes familiar with each participant's voice.

The <a href="#otterAi">automated meeting summaries</a> generate concise summaries of meeting content using AI — distilling a one-hour meeting's transcript into the key discussion points, decisions made, and context for someone who didn't attend. The summary generation runs automatically after the meeting completes without requiring manual note compilation. The <a href="#otterAi">action item extraction</a> identifies tasks, assignments, and commitments mentioned during the meeting — surfacing them as a structured list separate from the full transcript so follow-up items are visible and trackable without reading the complete recording.

<a href="#otterAi">Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet integration</a> connects Otter AI to the video conferencing platforms that professional teams use — automatically joining scheduled meetings, starting transcription when the meeting begins, and capturing the complete session without manual activation. Calendar integration reads meeting schedules and pre-populates connection details for automatic joining. <a href="#otterAi">Searchable transcript library</a> stores all meeting transcriptions in a searchable archive — finding specific discussions, decisions, or mentioned names across months of meetings through keyword search rather than rewatching recordings.

<a href="#otterAi">Collaborative note editing</a> allows multiple team members to edit, annotate, and add highlights to shared transcripts — building on the AI-generated base with human context, corrections, and additional notes. <a href="#otterAi">Export and sharing</a> delivers transcripts and summaries in multiple formats — sharing with team members who didn't attend, exporting to PDF or text for documentation, and integrating with productivity tools through available connections.
